Before publishing each article, verify:

- [ ] Article follows Marketing Content Style Guide
- [ ] All supporting links are valid and accessible
- [ ] SEO metadata completed (title, description, keywords)
- [ ] Social assets prepared (Twitter thread draft, LinkedIn excerpt)
- [ ] Newsletter feature written
- [ ] Internal links to related Salable content
- [ ] Code examples tested (where applicable)
- [ ] Images/diagrams created (where applicable)

## Flexibility Notes

This calendar represents the recommended schedule, but should adapt to:

**External Events**

- Major Stripe announcements
- Platform policy changes (Trello, Miro)
- Tax regulation updates
- Competitor moves

**Internal Events**

- Product launches may accelerate beta feature articles
- Customer feedback may inspire new topics
- High-performing content may warrant follow-ups

**Seasonal Considerations**

- Avoid major holidays for important content
- Year-end often sees increased SaaS evaluation
- Q1 budget cycles drive software purchasing

### X. Beyond Competitor Analysis: How Customer Research Shapes Sustainable Pricing

**Synopsis**
Traditional pricing strategies often rely too heavily on competitor analysis, risking commoditisation and leaving substantial revenue on the table. This article argues that truly sustainable pricing in SaaS and software hinges on rigorous, ongoing customer research—using empirical methods like Van Westendorp, MaxDiff, and value metric analysis—to understand willingness to pay, perceived feature value, and customer outcomes. Companies adopting these data-driven techniques routinely outperform peers in revenue growth, pricing power, and customer retention, making customer research the foundation for future-proof pricing.

**Lead Intro**
You’ve checked the going rates for your competitors’ subscriptions, maybe even copied them, but your growth has stalled—and so has your confidence. Every month feels like a gamble: if you raise prices, will customers churn? If you lower them, will you capture more, or just devalue what you’ve built? What if pricing isn’t a competitive guessing game at all? The most successful SaaS teams stopped benchmarking and started listening, turning real customer insight into pricing power. Their secret isn’t a spreadsheet; it’s a feedback loop, scientifically tuned to what customers genuinely value and are willing to pay—again and again.

**Key Takeaway**

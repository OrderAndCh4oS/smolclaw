# Beta Launch Announcement

This document defines the Salable Beta launch announcement article. The goal is to introduce the platform, highlight the key architectural shift, and drive sign-ups for the beta programme.

**Content Pillar**: Product Updates

**Publishing**: Launch day

## Article

### Introducing Salable Beta: Build the Pricing Model Your Business Actually Needs

**Synopsis**
Salable Beta launches with a fundamentally different approach to subscription billing. Instead of forcing one pricing model per plan, Salable Beta lets you combine flat-rate fees, per-seat charges, metered usage, and one-time payments within a single plan—all managed through a fully programmable API. This announcement introduces the platform, explains why the architecture matters, and invites early adopters to try it and share feedback.

**Lead Intro**
Most billing platforms force you to choose: flat-rate or usage-based, per-seat or metered. Salable Beta removes that constraint. You can now build pricing models that combine multiple charge types in a single plan, manage your entire pricing catalogue through the API, and handle team subscriptions without custom code. We're launching the beta today and inviting developers to try it, break it, and help us make it better.

**Target Audience**
SaaS Founder, Engineering Lead

**Key Takeaway**
Salable Beta's Line Item architecture lets you combine any pricing model in a single plan; the API gives you full control over products, plans, and entitlements; and it's ready for you to try today.

**Salable Hook**
Launch announcement; drives beta sign-ups and positions Salable as the next generation of subscription infrastructure

**Call to Action**
Sign up for the beta, explore the documentation, and share feedback on what's working and what isn't.

**Tone**
Confident but not boastful. We're proud of what we've built, but we're launching a beta because we want real-world feedback. Acknowledge this is the beginning, not the finished product.

**Structure**

1. Opening hook: What's different about Salable Beta (2-3 sentences)
2. The problem: Why current billing platforms constrain pricing strategy
3. The solution: Line Item architecture and API-first approach
4. Key capabilities: Brief highlights of what's possible (not exhaustive)
5. What we're looking for: Invitation to try and provide feedback
6. How to get started: Clear call to action

**What to Avoid**

- Exhaustive feature lists (save for documentation)
- Deep technical explanations (link to docs instead)
- Comparisons to competitors by name
- Overpromising on features still in development

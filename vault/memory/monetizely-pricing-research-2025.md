Monetizely Guide to SaaS Pricing Research (2025): Pricing research enables SaaS companies to quantify value, identify price sensitivity thresholds, and design packages reflecting how customers actually perceive/consume the product. Key findings: Value metric research is core—ProfitWell research shows alignment with customer success (not arbitrary metrics) correlates with 38% faster growth. Willingness-to-pay (WTP) research, feature value research, and customer segmentation are all critical for moving beyond copying competitors. OpenView: Companies running regular price optimization research outperform by 30% in revenue growth; however, <25% of SaaS companies actually conduct formal pricing research more than once per year, and those who review/prioritize at least quarterly grow 30% faster. Common pitfalls: bias from poor sampling, operational complexity, and neglecting post-launch feedback metrics.

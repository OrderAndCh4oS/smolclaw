#reference #pricing #customer research #behavioral economics #value-based

Simon-Kucher (2023): Behavioral and psychological pricing consulting experience demonstrates companies that design pricing based on deep customer research (willingness to pay, feature value scoring, segmentation) reliably see higher margins and improved net revenue retention. Simon-Kucher leverages academic and behavioral economics (such as the Van Westendorp model and MaxDiff analysis) to empirically link pricing tiers and bundles to what customers actually value, going well beyond market benchmarking. Customer-level offer design and regular testing drive sustainable, scientific pricing vs. reactive discounting or competitor-following.

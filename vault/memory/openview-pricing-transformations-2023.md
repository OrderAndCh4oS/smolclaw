OpenView: Your Guide to Pricing Transformations in 2023 — Authors: Kyle Poyar, Steven Forth. Publication: OpenView blog. Date: Jan 19, 2023. URL: https://openviewpartners.com/blog/your-guide-to-pricing-transformations-in-2023/ Key points: Hybrid pricing is rising; new OpenView survey data shows 46% of SaaS companies have adopted hybrid models (usage + subscription). Examples include Autodesk ‘Flex’ day-licenses and GitHub plans that include predetermined usage for Actions, Packages, Codespaces with overage pricing. Argues for a modern pricing tech stack to support complexity (metering, pricing ops). Predicts greater pricing complexity and more in-house pricing ownership in 2023.

OpenView Partners (Kyle Poyar, 2023), 'The Ultimate SaaS Pricing Resources Guide': Highlights the importance of aligning buyer personas and desired outcomes to the design of the pricing page and its CTAs. Recommends using visitor trust-building techniques, simplifying design, and offering free trials as effective CTAs to increase conversion. The guide emphasizes behavioral tactics: CTAs should be informative, reassuring, and reduce anxiety about next steps, especially on pricing pages where commitment feels riskier to users. Section: 'How to optimize your pricing page.'

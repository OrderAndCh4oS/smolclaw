Bessemer Venture Partners: Linear, volumetric, or bundling — Which type of usage-based pricing is right for you? Authors: Sameer Dholakia, Byron Deeter, Atlas Editors. Publication: BVP Atlas. Date: Jan 18, 2023. URL: https://www.bvp.com/atlas/linear-volumetric-or-bundling-which-type-of-usage-based-pricing-is-right-for-you Key points: Defines three UBP archetypes (linear per-unit, volumetric with allowances/overages/discount curves, and bundled where unit price increases with higher feature tiers). Notes adoption jump from 30% (2019) to 45% (2021); projected widespread adoption/test by 2023. Cites Bain that 90%+ of users who prefer subscriptions complain about UBP’s predictability; recommends tactics like pre-purchase, warnings. Pros/cons for predictability, margins, incentives. Relevant to developer tools where API calls, events, and seats interplay.

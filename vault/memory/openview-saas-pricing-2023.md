OpenView, Sarah DeVries & Sanji Johari, 2023: Over 94% of B2B SaaS pricing leaders update pricing and packaging at least once per year; 98% updated pricing or packaging since Sept 2022. Most mature SaaS companies shift to value-based pricing as they grow and gain resources to research value properly. Value-based pricing is a strategic differentiator and real growth lever, especially in tougher macroeconomic conditions. The most sophisticated companies refine value metrics, adjust packaging, and invest in research to understand what customers value most. Free trials and freemium strategies are commonly used to let customers experience value. Not innovating on monetization means falling behind. For smaller SaaS (as low as $1M ARR), value-based pricing is achievable without a giant in-house team.

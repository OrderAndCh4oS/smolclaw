#reference #pricing #cta #conversion #ab-testing

Baremetrics (Corey Haines, 2024) 'SaaS Pricing Models & Strategies Demystified': Stresses that pricing and packaging strategy must be continuously tested and iterated. CTAs such as free trial, demo, or buy now are central to activation models (how users begin engaging), and the messaging and placement of CTAs are critical for conversion. Pricing pages must be actionable—providing clear, direct next steps and reducing cognitive load. Minor tweaks in copy or CTA labelling can have significant impact on conversion rate, suggesting ongoing A/B testing of CTA text and placement is paramount for maximizing conversion.

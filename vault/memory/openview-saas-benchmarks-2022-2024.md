OpenView SaaS Benchmarks (2022-2024): Annual SaaS benchmarking reports reinforce that companies who frequently revisit and optimize pricing based on customer research (quarterly or better) outperform peers by up to 30% revenue growth. Sharp increases found in usage-based/value-based pricing models—companies using genuine customer value data (not just competitor benchmarking) are more resilient, especially amid cost inflation or market shocks. Key takeaway: the most sustainable pricing strategies are grounded in ongoing customer research, segmentation, and testing, rather than fixed competitor benchmarks.

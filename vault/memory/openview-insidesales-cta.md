OpenView Partners (Kyle Poyar, 2016) SaaS Pricing Best Practices: Case study of InsideSales.com shows improving conversion by changing the pricing page CTA from 'Let's Talk' to 'Try For Free' while lowering the barrier to a trial. The study found SaaS pricing pages are increasingly publishing prices and optimizing CTAs. Best-in-class pricing pages use clear, action-oriented CTAs and A/B testing to improve performance. Pages with explicit, low-friction CTAs (free trial, demo, buy now) outperform those with ambiguous or high-commitment language. Data from 87 SaaS companies shows the trend towards clear packaging, pricing communication, and simple, direct calls to action that reflect user intent and reduce friction.

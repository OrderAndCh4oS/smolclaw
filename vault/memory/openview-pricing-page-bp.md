#reference #pricing #cta #best-practices #conversion

OpenView Partners, '20 Pricing Page Best Practices That Will Increase Your Sales,' summary (2023): A top recommendation is tailoring CTAs to match user context: for example, 'Start your free trial' for SMB-focused SaaS and 'Contact sales' where deal customization is required. The effectiveness of a pricing page is largely determined by the user's ability to quickly understand pricing and next steps, with the CTA being most important. CTAs should reassure users, set expectations, and invite action with minimal risk. Poor CTA design (vagueness, mismatch to intent, or premature push to sign-up) leads to drop-off.

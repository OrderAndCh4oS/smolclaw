Baremetrics/Allison Barkley, 2026: Key implementation steps and benchmarks for value-based pricing in SaaS. Segmentation (by usage patterns, company size, and use case) is critical. Pricing metrics must reflect customer outcomes and be simple and measurable. Willingness to pay is best gauged by interviews, A/B tests, pilots, and conversion data. Feature mapping to customer value is driven by analyzing usage and identifying which features correlate to high LTV/customer retention. Metrics to monitor: churn (5–7%), CLV:CAC ratio (target 3:1), NRR (net revenue retention) by segment. Analytics tools like Baremetrics provide data to tailor and iterate pricing models as product-market fit and feature usage evolve.

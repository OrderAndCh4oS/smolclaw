#reference #pricing #usage-based #value-based #saas #openview #benchmark

OpenView, Kyle Poyar, 2023 SaaS Pricing Data: In 2023, SaaS pricing is getting more sophisticated—half of companies changed both pricing and packaging, and usage-based or hybrid pricing models are increasingly prevalent. 23% have usage-based subscription tiers, 18% are largely usage-based, 17% have tested usage-based pricing; only 42% have no UBP. Hybrid models that combine subscriptions and usage (e.g., per seat plus features based on actual consumption) are common, letting SaaS firms better match pricing to customer-perceived value as more usage and outcome data accrues. SaaS firms are bringing pricing strategy in-house, iterating more frequently, and using data to guide transitions. Early-stage firms iterate quickly; later-stage run deeper analyses. The evolution from flat-rate to value-based/usage-based is often staged and data-driven.

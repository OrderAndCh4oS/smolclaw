#reference #pricing #usage-based

Kyle Poyar, OpenView, Why is Usage-Based Pricing on the Rise? (Nov 2021):
- 45% of SaaS companies had some form of UBP in 2021, up from 34% in 2020. Trend expected to accelerate.
- Drivers: End-user era of SaaS, where buyers want to try and pay only for what they consume; traditional seat-based pricing does not reflect value for AI, automation, API products.
- UBP companies show higher net revenue retention (NRR), often exceeding 130% as in the example of Confluent.
- Investors have warmed to UBP, now preferring it in some S-1 filings.
- UBP sends a confidence signal to customers and pushes companies towards greater customer-centricity.

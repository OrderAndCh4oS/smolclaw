L.E.K. Consulting, 2025: Outcome-based and value-based pricing in SaaS now align cost with measurable results for customers, representing a move away from legacy models based on direct consumption or seats. This transition becomes possible as SaaS vendors accumulate more usage, outcome, and customer success data—not just raw usage but actual business results. Outcome-based models require close collaboration to define, measure, and track business value, but increase differentiation and stickiness. The switch from flat-rate is rarely all-at-once; companies typically experiment with new pricing for subsets of customers as data and confidence accrues.

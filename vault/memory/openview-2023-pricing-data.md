OpenView: What’s going on with pricing this year? A deep dive into 2023 pricing data — Author: Kyle Poyar. Publication: OpenView blog. Date: Nov 30, 2023. URL: https://openviewpartners.com/blog/2023-pricing-data/ Key findings (700+ SaaS cos): 50% changed pricing & packaging in 2023; 25% changed pricing only; 3% packaging only; 22% neither. Continued rise of complex/hybrid pricing; breakdown: 23% usage-based subscription tiers, 18% largely UBP, 17% tested UBP, 42% no UBP.

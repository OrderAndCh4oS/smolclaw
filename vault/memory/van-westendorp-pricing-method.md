#reference #pricing #customer research #van westendorp #pricing methods

Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Meter (methodology, cited in Monetizely and Simon-Kucher): This empirical customer research tool asks four framing questions about price perception to calculate an optimal pricing window reflecting willingness to pay. It's established as better than feature benchmarking/competitor copying, giving robust data on what real customers will pay and where price resistance sets in. Frequently cited as essential tool for customer-driven pricing.

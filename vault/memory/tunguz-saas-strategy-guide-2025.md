Tomasz Tunguz, 'A Founder's Guide to SaaS Strategy & Growth,' 2025: Describes the evolution of SaaS pricing strategies from traditional models (flat-rate, per seat) to value-based and usage-based pricing as companies scale. Value-based pricing is highlighted as optimal for high-ROI solutions but requires advanced customer understanding and usage data. Usage-based pricing is trending, especially in infrastructure SaaS, and is often a transitional step as more data accrues on actual customer value and outcomes. Pricing psychology, alignment of pricing metrics with perceived value, and iterative optimization are cited as crucial for moving beyond flat or basic tiered pricing.

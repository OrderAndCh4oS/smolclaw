ProfitWell's Patrick Campbell (2023-2024): Multiple expert interviews, including Intercom and Produx Labs, reinforce the pitfalls of competitor-focused pricing. Campbell asserts most SaaS companies underprice because they copy competitors or pick numbers at random without customer research. He recommends in-depth research into value metrics (how customers realize value), willingness to pay, and segment-specific drivers. His recurring message: 'Your price is the exchange rate on the value you’re providing.' Piloting, testing, and continuous customer interviews are essential for sustainable, growth-driving pricing. (References: Intercom podcast with Campbell, Produx Labs ep 104, Business of Software 2023 talk)

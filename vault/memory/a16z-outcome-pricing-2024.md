Andreessen Horowitz (Enterprise Newsletter): AI Is Driving A Shift Towards Outcome-Based Pricing — Authors: Ivan Makarov, James da Costa, Bobby Pinero. Publication: a16z Enterprise Newsletter. Date: Dec 19, 2024. URL: https://a16z.com/newsletter/december-2024-enterprise-newsletter-ai-is-driving-a-shift-towards-outcome-based-pricing/ Key points: AI reduces relevance of per-seat as software replaces labor; moves toward outcome or usage pricing. Variable inference costs push hybrid pricing. Examples: Zendesk moving from per-agent to per-resolution potential; AI-native companies using usage/outcome hybrids. Implication for developer tools: seats-only pricing underprices automation impact; need models aligned to outcomes/performance.

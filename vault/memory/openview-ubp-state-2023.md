#reference #pricing #usage-based

Kyle Poyar, OpenView, The State of Usage-Based Pricing: 2nd Edition (Feb 2023):
- 61% of SaaS companies expected to have adopted usage-based pricing (UBP) by end of 2023, with another 21% planning to test it in the future.
- Only 15% use a largely usage/pay-as-you-go model, but 46% use hybrid approaches, blending UBP with subscriptions.
- Examples: Apigee, Autodesk, HubSpot.
- Rise of new SaaS tooling for complex pricing stack (metering, billing, optimization).
- UBP aligns with product-led growth (PLG), tying revenue to actual value delivered.
- Usage-based companies shown to be more resilient and customer-centric.

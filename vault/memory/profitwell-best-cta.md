#reference #pricing #cta #conversion #profitwell

ProfitWell, 'Pricing Page Guide: What the Best 5 Pricing Pages Get Right' (2020): The CTA is critical to pricing page performance, acting as the conversion point. Effective pricing pages present clear, direct CTAs such as 'Start Free Trial' or 'Request a Demo', and ensure that CTA copy exactly matches the underlying user action to minimize friction. Low conversion is often traced to vague CTAs or unclear value proposition immediately associated with a CTA. Short CTAs that make commitment clear and feel low risk (free trial, see demo) outperform generic or high-friction alternatives.

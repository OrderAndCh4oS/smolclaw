Bain & Company: Is Consumption-Based Pricing Right for Your Software? — Authors: Simon Heap, Sukh Brar. Publication: Bain Technology Report 2022. Date: Sept 2022 (Tech Report 2022). URL: https://www.bain.com/insights/is-consumption-based-pricing-right-for-your-software-tech-report-2022/ Key data: Consumption-based software companies in 2021 grew revenue ~8 percentage points faster on average than license/subscription peers. Among highest-valuation software companies, those with consumption pricing averaged ~10 points higher NDR. 80% of customers report better value alignment with consumption pricing. Framework: assess right value meter, buyer preference, and required capabilities. Notes predictability concerns and need for guardrails (allowances, caps, burn-down).
